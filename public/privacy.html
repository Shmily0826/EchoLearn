Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 July 2026

EchoLearn ("we", "the app") is a YouTube/Bilibili-based English learning tool available at app.echo-learn.uk. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Account data: When you sign in with Google or email, we store your email address, display name, and a unique user ID (UID) via Firebase Authentication.

Learning data: Vocabulary words, sentences, study sessions, and daily plans you save within the app. This data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and optionally synced to Firebase Firestore under your UID.

Usage data: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking cookies. Server-side logs may record IP addresses and request timestamps for rate-limiting and security purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your learning data is used solely to provide the app's core features: spaced repetition review, progress tracking, and cross-device sync. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

Learning data is stored in your browser's localStorage and, if you are signed in, in Google Firebase Firestore (EU/US regions). Access is restricted to your own UID via Firestore Security Rules. API keys for AI and YouTube services are held server-side and never exposed to the client.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data persists as long as your account exists. You can delete your learning data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage. To request full account deletion (including Firebase data), contact us at 1014755473@qq.com.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and data storage. Firebase Privacy
  • DeepSeek API — AI-powered transcript analysis. Prompts contain transcript text only; no personal identifiers are sent.
  • YouTube / Bilibili — Video playback and transcript fetching. Subject to their respective terms.
  • Vercel — Hosting and serverless functions. Vercel Privacy

6. GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and port your personal data. Exercise these rights by contacting 1014755473@qq.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact
